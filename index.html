About

Orphan Waves is an experimental alt-rock outfit based in Helsinki Finland that enjoys balancing between atmospheric floatiness and energetic fireworks. The band consists of Markku Essel (guitar & vocals), Markus Kankkonen (bass, synth & backing vocals) and Janne Aspvik (drums).
